Pathway to Zero Natural Gas — Los Alamos Electrification Tools

Interactive results from a study of residential electrification in Los Alamos, NM: how the county's energy use could change, what it means for a single home, and what households say programs are worth.

Pick a tool. Each one runs entirely in your browser, updates as you change the inputs, and explains its assumptions. These are research tools for general information, not official utility tools.

About this project

These tools accompany the working paper Pathway to Zero Natural Gas, on the path to ending residential natural-gas use in Los Alamos. They turn the paper's main results — a household-anchored load projection, the measured effect of switching each appliance, and a survey choice experiment on willingness to pay — into calculators anyone can explore.
